How It Works

Offseason · Mar–Aug

Training Mondays, Tuesdays & Thursdays, 6:00–7:00 PM. Build strength, speed, and skills before the season.

Practices ramp up, teams are set, and equipment is fitted and handed out to athletes.

Games are played on Saturdays — cheer on the sidelines, and the whole Pack shows up.
