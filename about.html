About the Pack

Rome's youth football and cheerleading program — building athletes, character, and the future of Rome Wolves football.

Our Mission

Tougher Kids. Stronger Community.

We exist to give the kids of Rome a place to fall in love with the game — and to grow into confident, disciplined, hard-working young people along the way.

Football and cheer teach lessons that last a lifetime: show up, work hard, pick your teammate up, and never quit. Our volunteer coaches pour into every athlete who wears the crimson and gold, on the field and off it.

Fear the Pack isn't just a slogan. It's the standard we hold each other to.

What We Stand For

The Junior Wolves Way

Discipline

On time, prepared, and accountable — the habits that win games and build people.

Teamwork

One pack. We win together, lose together, and lift each other up.

Toughness

Grit and resilience — learning to push through hard things the right way.

Community

Rome takes care of its own. Families, sponsors, and coaches make it all possible.

The Bigger Picture

Feeding the Rome Wolves

The Junior Wolves are the official youth feeder program for the Rome High School and Rome Middle School Wolves. Same mascot. Same crimson and gold. Same Friday-night dream.

Every kid who starts here is taking a real step toward the field they've watched their heroes play on. We teach the systems, the culture, and the standard early — so when they reach middle and high school, they're ready.
