Who We Are

More than a game.
It's a pack.

The Rome Junior Wolves give kids in grades 1st–5th a place to learn football and cheer the right way — fundamentals, discipline, teamwork, and a whole lot of fun. We're a community-powered, volunteer-driven program with one goal: develop great young athletes and even better young people.

As the official youth feeder for the Rome High School and Rome Middle School Wolves, every Junior Wolf wears the same red and gold and chases the same dream — Friday nights under the lights.
