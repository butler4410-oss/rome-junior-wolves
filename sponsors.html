Beyond Sponsorship

Ways to Support the Pack

One-Time Gifts

Make a tax-deductible gift toward a specific need — equipment, helmets, travel, or apparel — or wherever it helps most. Email us to give.

Spirit Nights

Dine-out fundraisers at local partners like Shane's Rib Shack — eat well, give back. A percentage of proceeds supports the team.

Raffles

Seasonal prize raffles help fund equipment and field costs. Watch the News page for the next drawing.

Volunteer

Coaches, team parents, and game-day helpers keep the Pack running. A few hours makes a huge difference.

Let's Build Something for These Kids

Whether it's a sponsorship, a donation, or a few hours of your time — every contribution puts another kid on the field. We're a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, so your gift is tax-deductible.
